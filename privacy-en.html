1. Introduction

Family Bank ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: For authentication and login purposes
  • Family Name: Your family name
  • Children's Names: Names of children in the family
  • Financial Data: Balances, transactions, expenses, and income
  • Profile Pictures: Images you upload to the app (optional)
  • Usage Data: Actions you perform in the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide you with the app's services
  • Verify your identity and ensure security
  • Manage your family's financial data
  • Synchronize data between devices
  • Improve the app and provide technical support

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information is stored securely and used only for the purposes stated in this policy.

Information is shared only with:

  • Your family members (parents and children) who are authorized to access the family account
  • Technical service providers (such as MongoDB Atlas, Railway) required to operate the app

5. Data Security

We use advanced technical and administrative security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Two-factor authentication via SMS
  • Limited data access only to authorized users
  • Regular data backups

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request to see the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information through the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your family account and all associated data
  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your information (if not required for service operation)

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your family account is active. If you delete your account, all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the last updated date. We recommend that you review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@familybank.app
  • GitHub: Report an Issue
